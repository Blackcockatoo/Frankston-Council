How to use

Open, print, test, and let the city answer back.

SVG identity pack

Use the independent Frankston 2035 mark, not the council logo.

The logo/SVG pack contains editable vector lockups, PNG exports, social cards, favicons, colour tokens, and reference-only council logo images for comparison. The active public identity is the Frankston 2035 mark.

Which file to use

  • Website header: transparent lockup SVG or PNG.
  • Poster footer: dark lockup PNG for light posters, light lockup PNG for dark posters.
  • Small icon: standalone mark or favicon.
  • Documents: stacked lockup on covers, wordmark inside pages.
  • Do not use: the reference-only Frankston City logo files unless council has approved that use.
