Wall Art Pack

Ten display-ready artworks for civic walls.

A display-ready A4 artwork set for walls, windows, classrooms, community tables, and local conversations. Each poster uses the arched pedestrian bridge over Kananook Creek as Frankston's civic emblem.

See Poster Seeds instead

Civic emblem

Kananook Creek bridge. Not a lighthouse.

Each artwork uses the arched pedestrian bridge over Kananook Creek as the key civic emblem — a locally specific Frankston landmark, not a generic coastal icon. The bridge connects community, place, and creative exchange.

How to use

Display, discuss, teach.

  • Print A4 portrait on standard laser or inkjet paper.
  • Laminate for window or outdoor display.
  • Use as a discussion starter for community tables, school briefings, and pilot conversations.
  • Combine with the Lesson Pack Starter for a 30-minute classroom session.
  • Do not imply Frankston City Council endorsement without written approval.
