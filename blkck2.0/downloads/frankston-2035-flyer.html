Frankston 2035 - Art That Lives

The greatest public artwork is not a mural. It is a community that learns to create.

What

A civic creativity platform connecting public art, schools, libraries, families, artists, and healthy technology.

First move

Start with one low-risk pilot: a library creative pack, school pathway, or small public-art prompt.

Why

Frankston becomes more creative when people are invited to make, not just look.

Next step

Pick one pilot, one host setting, one review path, and one 30/60/90 test.
