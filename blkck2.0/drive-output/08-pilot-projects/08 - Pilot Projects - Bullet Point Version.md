# 08 - Pilot Projects
## Bullet Point Version

## Core rule

- Pick one pilot first.
- Do not bundle all five.
- A pilot must be explainable, testable, and stoppable.

## Pilot 1 - Living Mural / QR Public Art Layer

- One existing mural, foreshore artwork, or location.
- One QR marker.
- One no-login web layer.
- One learning activity.
- Anonymous aggregate usage only.
- Optional classroom extension.
- Best first candidate.

## Pilot 2 - Library Creative Pack

- Printable sheets.
- Colouring/making activity.
- Short song or audio layer.
- QR links.
- Low setup cost.
- Library event option.
- Best backup if outdoor/site permissions are slow.

## Pilot 3 - Frankston x Susono Classroom Exchange Pack

- English/Japanese starter resources.
- Song/chant/pronunciation layer.
- Student artwork exchange concept.
- Teacher-friendly format.
- No claim of official Susono participation unless confirmed.

## Pilot 4 - Healthy Technology Mini-App

- Short session.
- No login.
- No personal data.
- No infinite scroll.
- No dark patterns.
- Offline making prompt.
- Family/classroom mode.

## Pilot 5 - Respect Through Participation Pathway

- Process pilot, not content pilot.
- Traditional Owner engagement pathway.
- Listening and protocol.
- No invented language, symbols, stories, or cultural claims.
- Possible future outputs only after guidance.

## What Council may need to provide

- One contact.
- One candidate site/program area.
- Permission pathway advice.
- Privacy/accessibility expectations.
- Review of public language.

## What Blue Snake Studio may provide

- Prototype.
- Draft copy.
- Static web layer.
- Printable activity.
- QR concept.
- Feedback summary.

## Decision needed

- Which one pilot should be scoped first?
