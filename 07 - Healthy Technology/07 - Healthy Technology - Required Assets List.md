## Example copy assets

### Privacy line

This activity does not ask children for names, accounts, contact details, profiles, or personal information.

### Exit line

You have the prompt. Close the screen and make it offline.

### Classroom line

Use this as a group activity. Students do not need individual accounts.

### Family line

Open the prompt together, then look up, walk, draw, or talk.

### Council line

This pilot uses no-login public access, offline prompts, and anonymous aggregate measures only where needed.

## Asset risks

Do not create:

- fake privacy assurance
- vague consent language
- decorative cultural symbols
- child-facing upload prompts without review
- engagement badges that mimic addictive app patterns
- "download our app" barriers
- complex dashboards before the pilot needs them
