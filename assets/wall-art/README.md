# Wall Art Pack — Frankston 2035

Ten A4 civic artwork posters for walls, windows, classrooms, community tables, discussion packs, and seed-kit displays.

## Civic emblem

Every artwork uses the **arched pedestrian bridge over Kananook Creek in Frankston** as the key civic emblem.
This is a locally specific landmark — not a lighthouse, not a generic coastal icon.
The bridge connects community, place, and creative exchange.

## Format

- A4 portrait (210mm × 297mm), 595 × 842px at 72 dpi / 2480 × 3508 at 300 dpi for print
- Dark-gold civic aesthetic: deep ink background, gold type, Kananook Creek bridge arch
- Safe to print on standard laser or inkjet

## Intended uses

- Classroom and school corridor walls
- Library windows and table displays
- Community hall and civic building noticeboards
- Discussion packs for council briefings and pilot conversations
- Seed-kit handouts at events and community tables
