Civic idea

Frankston is the hero.

This is not a generic tourism wrapper and not fake official branding. It is a living civic arts system: posters, learning tools, public storytelling, local memory, maker pathways, workshops, and shareable culture designed to make Frankston feel seen, specific, and alive.

Stage one

Launch small. Make it undeniable.

  1. Create the public-facing one-page pitch and print pack.
  2. Build a visual culture library: pier, foreshore, station, schools, shops, makers, elders, kids.
  3. Test one QR-linked civic art trail with simple participation and feedback loops.
  4. Use the evidence to ask for the next room, not the whole city at once.
