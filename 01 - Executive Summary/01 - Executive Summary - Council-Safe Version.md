# 01 — Executive Summary
## Council-Safe Version

Suitable for direct inclusion in council briefing material without further political review.

---

**Proposal in brief**

Blue Snake Studio, a Frankston-based creative-technology studio, proposes a staged, low-cost pilot program that would add an interactive, educational layer to a small number of Frankston's existing public art assets. The underlying technology has already been designed and tested by the studio independently of this proposal (see Section 04 for portfolio detail).

**What is explicitly NOT being proposed at this stage**

- No request for ongoing or multi-year funding commitment.
- No claim of an existing First Nations partnership — any related content is framed strictly as an invitation for future, consent-based collaboration, entirely on Traditional Owners' terms.
- No claim of an existing formal program with Susono — any bilingual content is a proposal for consideration by the relevant sister-city contacts, not a description of current activity.
- No criticism, implied or otherwise, of Council's existing public art, foreshore, or education investment. This proposal is explicitly structured to build on that investment.
- No exclusive or long-term procurement commitment to Blue Snake Studio. Each stage is independently fundable and independently able to be discontinued by Council.

**What is being asked of Council at this stage**

To note this Executive Summary and the accompanying Full Proposal, and — if there is interest — to indicate support for scoping a single, modest, time-boxed Stage One pilot (one location, one school term, bounded one-off cost, no ongoing commitment).

**Risk position**

All costs are staged and re-approved at each stage (see Roadmap, Section 00). No personal data is collected from children beyond anonymous, aggregated usage metrics. All cultural content involving First Nations or Susono material requires explicit, ongoing, and revocable consent from the relevant community before publication.

**Supporting evidence of capability**

Section 04 (Blue Snake Studio) documents the studio's existing portfolio of built and tested creative-technology projects, offered as evidence that the proposed pilot is technically achievable without speculative development cost.
