Image rules

Record owner, location, date, caption, permission status, and alt text for every image before use.

  • No image implies council approval, First Nations consent, or Susono participation unless formally confirmed.
  • No student faces or community photos without explicit consent and release.
  • Do not publish archive material without confirming copyright and public-use status.
  • Label all draft/campaign images as such in internal documentation.
  • Replace draft images with real approved Frankston photography before any formal public launch.
