# 08 - Pilot Projects
## Purpose Statement

This chunk turns Frankston 2035 - Art That Lives from a civic vision into testable first moves. Every pilot must be small enough to scope in 30/60/90 days, honest enough to evaluate, and safe enough not to overclaim cultural, council, school, or sister-city support.

The pilots are not a rollout plan. They are options for choosing one Stage One test.

---

## The five pilot options

1. Living Mural / QR Public Art Layer
2. Library Creative Pack
3. Frankston x Susono Classroom Exchange Pack
4. Healthy Technology Mini-App
5. Respect Through Participation Pathway

---

## Hard boundary

The fifth pilot is a process pilot, not a content pilot. It must not create First Nations language, symbols, stories, histories, or place-based learning content unless appropriate Traditional Owner organisations guide and permit that work.
