## Rules before printing anything publicly

1. Remove any watermark or placeholder text.
2. Confirm all email addresses and URLs are live.
3. Add the disclaimer: "Independent proposal seed by Blue Snake Studio. Not official Frankston City Council branding or an endorsed council program unless formally approved."
4. Check consent and image rights for any photographs included.
5. Run teacher/principal review before distributing to students.
6. Do not print QR codes until destination pages are stable and tested.
