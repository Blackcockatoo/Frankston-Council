# 04 — Blue Snake Studio
## 1-Page Summary

---

**The question this answers:** Can Blue Snake Studio actually build what Section 01 describes, or is this just a pitch?

**The short answer:** Yes — most of the hard parts are already built and tested, independently of this proposal.

**The existing body of work:**
- **Digital DNA Laboratory** — a working interactive learning experience (five play modes: helix, mandala, particle field, sound, journey). Proves browser-based interactive learning is a solved problem here.
- **Nara's Colour Quest** — a kid-safe, privacy-first learning site. Proves child safety is a design default, not an add-on.
- **Hepta Lock** — a tested, verified logic puzzle engine. Proves the studio ships rigorous interaction, not just visuals.
- **Moss60** — an original base-60 mathematical system underlying everything above. Proves "art that teaches maths" has real substance, not just marketing language.
- **Black Wing Crew / Frankston–Susono exchange** — an existing cultural project connecting Frankston and Susono. Proves the sister-city bridge Section 05 proposes building on is real, not hypothetical.

**What this is not:** None of these were built specifically for Council, and none would deploy unmodified into a pilot. This section proves capability and philosophy — genuine adaptation work is still required.

**Why it matters for risk:** Funding the adaptation of proven capability is a materially smaller risk than funding the invention of something new. The hard problems — interactivity, child privacy, tested logic, real mathematical content — are already solved.

**The bridge:** Section 01's low-cost pilot pathway is the proportionate next step for proving adaptation, not invention, is what Frankston is funding.
