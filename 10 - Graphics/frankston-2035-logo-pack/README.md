# Frankston 2035 — Art That Lives Logo Pack

Independent civic-art identity pack for the Frankston 2035 project.

## Included

- SVG lockups: horizontal, stacked, wordmark, standalone mark
- PNG exports: light, dark, transparent
- Social icons: 1080x1080 square and 1200x630 Open Graph cards
- Favicons: SVG, PNG sizes, and ICO
- Web manifest
- Palette JSON/CSS
- Reference-only folder with the Frankston City logo images supplied by Tom

## Brand safety note

The official Frankston City logo files in `reference-only/` are included only as visual reference DNA.
Do not use them on Frankston 2035 posters, public assets, websites, or flyers unless Frankston City Council has approved that usage.
The active campaign identity in this pack is the independent Frankston 2035 mark: bay wave, pier line, sunrise/sand, and foreshore strip.

## Colour DNA

- Bay teal: #087F9B
- Deep bay: #005B73
- Sea glass: #58B8C9
- Sand gold: #F6C650
- Foreshore green: #12A56A
- Concrete: #E8ECEF

- Website header: `svg/frankston-2035-lockup-transparent.svg`
