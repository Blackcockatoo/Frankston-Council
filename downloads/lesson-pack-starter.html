Frankston 2035: Notice, Make, Share

A 30-minute classroom prompt for public art, place, and healthy technology. No login. No student personal data. Create offline first.

1. Notice

Choose one local place, artwork, photo, or memory. What details do people usually miss?

2. Make

Create a sketch, title, short poem, map, pattern, or poster line that helps someone else notice it.

3. Share safely

Display in class or library. Do not publish names, faces, or student work publicly without consent.

Teacher note

This is a starter sheet only. Adapt for age, school policy, accessibility, and local permissions.
