# Foundation — Memetic / High-Impact Version

For posters, social tiles, pull-quotes, and the opening line of any speech or deck.

---

**Primary line (the one to use most):**

> Art that stops at the wall is finished. Art that lives keeps going.

**Alternates, by context:**

- *For education-facing material:* Every child should leave school having made something the city can see — not just seen something the city made.
- *For community-facing material:* A mural is a moment. A living mural is a relationship.
- *For council-facing material:* We're not asking Frankston to imagine the future — we're asking it to build a small piece of it and see what happens.
- *For First Nations / respectful-process material:* Respect doesn't scale by being loud. It scales by being asked first.
- *For the project name itself, as a standalone tile:* **Frankston 2035 — Art That Lives.**

**Usage rule:** one memetic line per page or tile, set large, in the serif display face, on a `paper` or `sand` background, with no competing text. Let it breathe.
