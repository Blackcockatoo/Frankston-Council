# Foundation — Council-Safe Version

This is the version of the project foundation suitable for direct inclusion in council briefing material, without further editing for political risk.

---

**What is being proposed**

A staged program, led by Blue Snake Studio, to add an interactive, educational layer to Frankston's existing public art and creative culture — built on technology the studio has already designed and tested, at modest and clearly staged cost.

**What this is not**

- Not a claim of an existing First Nations partnership. Any First Nations content is, and will remain, an invitation to Traditional Owners to guide whether, how, and when any collaboration occurs — entirely on their terms, including the right to decline.
- Not a claim of an existing formal program with Susono. The Frankston–Susono sister-city relationship is real; any specific bilingual or exchange activity proposed here is a suggestion for consideration by the relevant sister-city contacts, not a description of work already underway.
- Not a criticism of Council's existing investment in public art, foreshore activation, or education. The proposal is explicitly designed to build on that investment.
- Not a request for long-term exclusive procurement commitment. Each stage is independently fundable and independently able to be discontinued.

**How risk is managed**

- Costs are staged — see the Roadmap document — with each stage requiring a fresh, informed decision by Council.
- No data is collected from children beyond anonymous, aggregated usage counts (e.g. QR scans).
- All cultural content involving First Nations or Susono material is subject to explicit, ongoing consent from the relevant community before publication, and can be withdrawn at the request of that community at any time.

**What Council is being asked to do at this stage**

Nothing more than note this proposal and, if there is interest, agree to the Stage One pilot scope and budget described in the Roadmap document — a single location, a single school term, a clearly bounded cost.
