# Risk Register
## Frankston 2035 - Art That Lives

This register is deliberately blunt. A proposal that cannot name its risks is not ready for council, community, schools, or funders.

## Risks that could kill the proposal

1. Any implied First Nations approval that does not exist.
2. A public-facing ask that sounds like "fund the whole vision."
3. A council reader seeing no concrete pilot, owner, or decision.
4. Child privacy or attention-design failure.
5. Blue Snake Studio appearing as the hero instead of Frankston.
6. Unsupported claims being read as proof.
