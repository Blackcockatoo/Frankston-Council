---

## Before any QR code is printed on a public sign

1. **Stable URL confirmed.** The destination page is live, tested, and not expected to change path.
2. **Privacy note visible.** The linked page includes a plain-English note about what data (if any) is collected. For this project: no accounts, no child data, no analytics that identify individuals.
3. **No-login access.** The page works without creating an account or logging in.
4. **Print fallback exists.** A printable worksheet or prompt is available as a fallback if the QR scan fails.
5. **Tested on two phones.** The QR code has been scanned successfully on at least two different devices.
6. **Clear ending.** The linked page makes it obvious when the session is done: a closing line, an offline action, no infinite scroll.
7. **Sign disclaimer.** The physical sign includes: project name ("Frankston 2035"), the independent proposal disclaimer, and a contact email.
8. **Permission confirmed.** The artwork, site, and school permissions are all documented before the sign is installed.
